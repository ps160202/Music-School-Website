About Treble Music School

This school of music was founded in 2011 by Mr. Vedant Mishra, a great pianist and succesful buisnessman. Mr Mishra used to teach students at his houde until he founded this school for about 15 years.

We aim to help and guide students around their instrument and help them excel and learn to their best. We also believe in one-to-one sessions so that there is enough attention on every student.
